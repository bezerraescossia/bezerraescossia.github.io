In today's digital age, the volume, variety, and velocity of data generated are unprecedented. This abundance of data presents a significant challenge: making sense of it all. Businesses are drowning in a sea of unstructured information, struggling to derive actionable insights to drive decision-making, optimize processes, and gain a competitive edge. The problem lies not just in the accumulation of data but in its transformation into meaningful intelligence that fuels innovation and growth.

As a Data Engineer, I'm deeply immersed in tackling this challenge head-on. My professional journey includes experiences as a consultant in some of Brazil's leading telecommunications companies, where I learned to navigate complex environments and propose innovative solutions to emerging challenges. Currently, I have the honor of being part of an international team at IBM, where I play a crucial role in data management and analysis, contributing to process optimization and the development of effective strategies.

My professional journey has also been enriched by global experiences, having lived and worked in both England and Germany. Part of my academic background includes a sandwich degree program in Germany, broadening my perspective and reinforcing my ability to adapt to different cultures and work environments.

Furthermore, I am driven by curiosity and enthusiasm for Data Science and Data Engineering, always seeking to learn and apply new techniques and tools to drive innovation and growth. I am committed to continuing my journey of success, tackling challenges with determination and creativity, and contributing to the advancement of the data field on a global scale.
